Mysteries & Thrillers · Lauren Beukes · 2013

The Shining Girls

“The girl who wouldn’t die hunts the killer who shouldn’t exist.”

Harper Curtis is a killer who stepped out of the past. Kirby Mazrachi is the girl who was never meant to have a future. Kirby is the last shining girl — one of the bright young women, burning with potential, whose lives Harper is destined to snuff out. After a tumultuous childhood, Kirby finds herself the only one of Harper’s victims to survive, and sets out to bring her would-be killer to justice.
